1. What is Capital Shop?

Capital Shop is an e-commerce platform designed to provide a seamless shopping experience. It's also a portfolio project showcasing the developer's expertise in web development.

No, this is a portfolio project and does not process real transactions. All payment features are for demonstration purposes only.
